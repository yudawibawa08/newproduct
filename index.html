The Craft

From Nature to Your Serenity

Selected Bamboo Sticks

Made from high-quality bamboo that is naturally dried. These sticks are sturdy, produce minimal ash, and emit no burnt-wood odor when lit.

Spike Powder

Functions as a natural binder without synthetic chemicals. This wood powder ensures a stable, even burn and keeps the incense lit without overpowering the core aroma.

Pure Essential Oils

Pure oil extracts from flowers and plants, these oils absorb perfectly into the incense fibers, providing a clean, therapeutic, and respiratory-safe calming effect.

Coconut Shell Charcoal

Ensures a burn without thick black smoke. Coconut shell charcoal makes the incense smoke smoother, toxin-free, and safe for indoor use or your meditation spaces.

Burn Time

45 to 60 minutes per stick, depending on your room's air circulation.

Natural

100% free from chemical dips and artificial colorants, making it safe for daily use.
